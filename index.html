Bio

I'm Shaurya Govil — an Associate at Boston Consulting Group, and a graduate of the Indian Institute of Technology. I like getting into the details of hard problems and figuring out what actually moves the needle.

I'm drawn to work that challenges me to think clearly and act decisively — whether that's inside a consulting firm or somewhere more scrappy.
